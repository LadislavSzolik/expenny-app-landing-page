FAQs

Where will the data I enter the app be stored?

All the data you enter remains only on your device. There are no trackers. In the future I plan to release an option to sync between your devices.

Is it free?

Yes. There are many expense tracking apps available on App Store, but I like to develop apps and I wanted to create one that specifically suits my needs.

What about Android and other platforms?

For now it is only for iOS.

Are there any integrations?

Not yet.

Can I import expenses from Excel sheet?

Not yet. It is planned for the app to have an option to export and import CSV file.
